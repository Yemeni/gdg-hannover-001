Arkadia.dev - Building Better Apps

Founded in 2018, Arkadia Studio is Yemen's first design and development studio, specializing in:

  • App Development
  • Game Development
  • Graphic Design
  • Gamification

Our Mission

To create authentic Yemeni-made content that inspires, educates, and empowers. We aim to spread culture, promote peace, and provide opportunities for young talent.

Our Vision

To lead the gaming and app development industry in Yemen and the Middle East, while showcasing the beauty of Yemeni culture to the world.
